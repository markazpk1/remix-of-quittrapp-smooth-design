

## SaaS Landing Page — Quittrapp-Style

A modern, dark-themed landing page with purple accent colors, smooth scroll animations, and polished UI — matching the look and feel of Quittrapp.com.

### Design System
- **Dark background** (#0a0a0f) with subtle gradients
- **Purple accent** color (violet/purple glow effects)
- Light text on dark, smooth font sizing
- Glassmorphism cards with subtle borders
- Scroll-triggered fade-in animations throughout

### Sections (in order)

1. **Navbar** — Logo, nav links (Features, Pricing, Blog, Support), CTA button with purple glow

2. **Hero** — Social proof badge ("Trusted by X+ users"), bold headline, subtitle, two CTA buttons (primary + secondary), hero image/mockup on the right with purple glow effect

3. **Logo Bar** — "As Featured In" scrolling marquee of brand logos

4. **Stats** — 3 animated counters (e.g., "10K+ Users", "99.9% Uptime", "4.9★ Rating")

5. **How It Works** — Alternating left/right feature cards with images and descriptions (4 items), with subtle entrance animations

6. **Creator/Endorsement Section** — Grid of creator/endorser cards with photos, names, and social handles

7. **Testimonials** — Horizontal scrolling review cards with star ratings, user photos, quotes

8. **Features Grid** — Bento-style grid of feature cards with icons/images, hover effects

9. **Pricing** — 2-3 tier pricing cards with purple highlight on recommended plan

10. **FAQ** — Accordion-style frequently asked questions

11. **Final CTA** — Bold headline with download/signup button, purple gradient background

12. **Footer** — Links, social icons, copyright

### Animations & Polish
- Intersection Observer for scroll-triggered fade-in/slide-up on all sections
- Smooth hover effects on cards and buttons
- Purple glow/gradient effects on key elements
- Counter animation on stats when they scroll into view
- Marquee animation on logo bar
